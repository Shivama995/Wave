About Us
LGBT has become a widely accepted designation for minorities based on sexual and gender orientation. All members of these subgroups are subject to similar prejudices rooted in beliefs and traditions about sexuality and gender. LGBT is an initialism that stands for lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender. The initialism LGBT is intended to emphasize a diversity of sexuality and gender identity-based cultures.To recognize this inclusion, a popular variant adds the letter Q for those who identify as queer and/or are questioning their sexual identity as LGBTQ, recorded since 1996.This was soon followed by bisexual and transgender. people also seeking recognition as legitimate categories within the larger community.
The term trans* has been adopted by some groups as a more inclusive alternative to "transgender", where trans (without the asterisk) has been used to describe trans men and trans women, while trans* covers all non-cisgender (genderqueer) identities, including transgender, transsexual, transvestite, genderqueer, genderfluid, non-binary, genderfuck, genderless, agender, non-gendered, third gender, two-spirit, bigender, and trans man and trans woman.

Lifestyle
Every year in the month of February, thousands of people gather and celebrate LGBT pride by rallying on the streets and hoping for society to accept them in every state of the India. The LGBT community faces a lot of problems.
The main problem is acceptance from people outside the community.
  • Marginalization is at the core of exclusion from fulfilling and full social lives at individual, interpersonal and societal levels.
  • LGBT students face harassment in schools. Being a teenager is tough enough without fearing harassment in a place where you‘re supposed to feel safe.
  • The myriad problems facing LGBT people who are homeless include a lack of housing and services that meet their specific needs.
Impact of Exclusion and Discrimination:
  • Dropping out of school earlier
  • Unable to find regular jobs, have less options than others.
  • Decide to follow their parents to marry opposite sex and then divorce.
  • Attempt suicide
